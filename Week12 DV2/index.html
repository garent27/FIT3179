Competition Activity

This heatmap reveals the explosive growth of competitions across continents since 2010. The deeper blue shade indicates higher event numbers per region yearly. Observe the remarkable expansion in Asia and North America and the powerful rebound following 2021.

The geographic distribution demonstrates how speedcubing evolved from a niche hobby to a global phenomenon. The recovery patterns show community resilience and increased investment in official WCA events worldwide.

Gender Participation Over Time

These charts track male and female participation growth across the most popular events. Each stacked bar displays total competitor numbers, showing explosive overall sport growth and the consistent gender ratio within the community. The 3x3 Cube dominates with the largest field.

The data reveals speedcubing's inclusive competitive culture. While more males participate overall, the stable ratio over time demonstrates how community efforts have fostered sustained female participation growth.

New Competitors Growth

This chart visualizes the annual influx of first-time competitors, revealing geographic waves of sport popularity. The stacked areas show how continents have contributed to community growth over time. Notice the dramatic surge from Asia and Europe post-2022, signaling powerful global expansion after pandemic disruptions.

The visualization captures speedcubing's global momentum and regional leadership evolution. The remarkable post-2022 acceleration shows competitions have not only recovered but are attracting unprecedented new participants worldwide.
